After the pharmacy practice and business ownership, Matthew developed passion and skills in statistics and data analytics as a software product manager and a pharmacy informaticist to advance the patientcare through providing both clinical and operational insights and recommendations from analyses of large Electronic Medical Records (EMR) databases, mostly focused on drug use evaluation, billing and financial reports, and clinical or operational measures and recommendations.
